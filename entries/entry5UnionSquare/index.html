Start the day with some boba from FiftyLan - go for the golden milk tea, it's a hit. Then, head over to Spicy Moon, a cool vegan spot near Washington Square, and grab lunch. Don't miss the Dan Dan noodles and chili wontan oil - they're awesome. Afterward, check out the Strand Bookstore. Maybe snag one of their cute tote bags while you're at it. Then, do some eco-friendly shopping at thrift stores like Beacon's Closet, Crossroads, and 2nd Street NYC - it's a fun trip down memory lane. Finish off the day with some takeaway dinner from Dainobu, a Japanese grocery store. Enjoy your meal and chat about the day's adventures in the heart of NYC's Union Square.
